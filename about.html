About STI Academy

STI stands for School of Technology and Innovation. It was created to make tech education simple, practical, and affordable for everyone.

Many people want to learn tech skills but do not know where to start. We solve that by teaching step by step in a clear and beginner friendly way.

Our mission is to help young people gain skills that can help them earn online and get jobs

At STI Academy, we guide you from beginner to job ready level.
